Active listening skills



Active listening is the ability to focus completely on the person who is talking, understand their message, comprehend the information and respond thoughtfully. Active listeners use verbal and nonverbal techniques to show and keep their attention on the one who is communicating. Developing and using active listening skills can show your colleagues that you are engaged and have an interest in the project or task at hand. 



Related listening skills include:


  • Asking questions
  • Note-taking
  • Organization
  • Punctuality
  • Verbal/nonverbal communication
