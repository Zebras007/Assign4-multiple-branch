Customer service skills



Customer service skillsare traits and practices that help you address customer needs to create a positive experience. In general, customer service skills rely heavily on problem-solving and communication. Customer service is often considered a “soft skill,” including traits like active listening and reading both verbal and nonverbal cues. 



Related listening skills include:


  • Active listening
  • Empathy
  • Interpersonal skills
  • Problem-solving
  • Reliability
